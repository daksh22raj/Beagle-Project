Beagle Trivia

  1. Beagles are scent hounds, developed primarily for hunting hare.
  2. They have one of the best-developed senses of smell among dog breeds.
  3. Beagles are intelligent, but can be stubborn and difficult to train.
  4. They are friendly, curious, and make excellent family pets.
